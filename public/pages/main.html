What is ColLab?

ColLab is the place made by Italians for Italians to help startups grow!

Here, you can find people interested in your startup, such as businesses and investors. Just create a profile for your startup and wait for people to contact you!



Our vision

ColLab is not just a place for startuppers. We wanted to make a place where startups can gain visibility to find people interested in the project. That's what this platform is for: finding projects you like and incorporate it into your business, or sharing your idea to find people that believe in it and can help you with it.
Here, businesses can find startups that offer innovative services that can help them. This way both profit from it!
But, at least here in Italy, a single place to connect businesses and startups to allow users to find what they need was lacking. And that's where the idea of ColLab came from!
