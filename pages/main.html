ColLab

The place to help your startup grow!


What is ColLab?

ColLab is the place made by Italians for Italians to help startups grow!

  • Have an amazing idea but don't know how to make it into a reality? Find the help you need by searching ColLaborators on our website!
  • Want to offer your skills for someone else's ideas? Become a ColLaborator!
  • Interested in finding ideas by other people you can join, invest in, or offer help? Browse other people's startups!


Our vision

ColLab is not just a place for startuppers. We wanted to make a place where anyone can contribute in some way: if you don't have a startup idea of your own but would love to meet new people and collaborate to a project, this is the place for you! That's what this platform is for: finding projects you'd like to join and offer your help; or sharing your idea to find people that believe in it and can help you with it.
So anyone can help, because everyone has something to offer: ideas, skills, investments, or anything more you can think of!
But, at least here in Italy, a single place to join all of these factors and allow users to find what they need was lacking. And that's where the idea of ColLab came from!
